ABOUT VTIC

Vocational Training Institute fro Champions (VTIC) is a Vocational and Certification Center found in Buea, Cameroon. It provides a conducive and enabling environment for students and professional to upgrade their skills and equally learn new skills.
VTIC have 100% practicing professionals in her teaching list. Founded in September, 2023 by Mr. Amungwa Gerald a seasoned educationist who worked as pioneered Principal of Champions Comprehensive High School Mile VI, Buea, Head of Marketing Department FOMIC Polytechnic, Director of Markeing, Biaka University Institute of Buea and has taught Business related courses in more than 15 Higher Institutions in Cameroon. Amungwa Gerald is equally the founded and CEO of Low Cost Centre specialized in General Contracts and Supplies.


Amungwa Gerald was highly motivated to start a professional education after a critical evaluation of his students upon graduation. Most of the students upon graduation from either state universities in Cameroon or most professional schools faced a lot of difficulties in gaining employment in standards corporations or to startup businesses and a few students who picked up employment opportunities or started up businesses needed a vocational center to upgrade their skills to perfectly match with the job market. It is pivoted to remark that the job market is constantly changing thus, skills learn today may not be needed tomorrow therefore a centre of this nature will constantly innovate to bring new training to match with the current and future job market skills. VTIC is offer Digital Marketing , Web Design, Graphic Design, Video Editing and General Microsoft Office Packages in her school of Information Technology. In the school of Business Management VTIC specializes in Event Management , Real Estate Management and Business Coaching . BOARD OF DIRECTORS

The board of Directors is the highest decision organ of Vocational Training Institute for Champions. They securitize strategic decisions of the Director and advise him on key decisions of the institution and related businesses of VTIC. The decision to dismiss key personnel such as strategic managers must be approved by the board, and the board must approve key expansion projects of VTIC.

Why Choose Us

VTIC offers a range of programs, tailored to different learning styles and time commitments. Whether you need a quick start or a comprehensive dive into your chosen field, you will find a program that suits your needs. Plus, VTIC offers affordable tuition, making quality vocational training accessible to everyone.

VTIC boasts a team of experienced and passionate instructors who are committed to your success. They bring industry knowledge and real-world experience to the classroom, ensuring you learn relevant and up-to-date skills. Plus, VTIC fosters strong connections with businesses, offering valuable networking opportunities and potential career pathways. .

VTIC emphasizes practical skills and hands-on experience. You will not just be memorizing theory; you will be applying your knowledge in real-world projects. This approach ensures you graduate with the skills and confidence to excel in your chosen field.
