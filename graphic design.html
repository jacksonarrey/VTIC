GRAPHIC DESIGN

GRAPHIC DESIGN IN VTIC

At our school, we believe that graphic design is more than just a career; it’s a powerful means of communication and a vital skill in today’s visually-driven world. Our passion for fostering creativity and innovation drives us to offer comprehensive programs that equip students with the tools they need to succeed in this dynamic field

Our Comprehensive Graphic Design Programs

3-Month Program:

Ideal for those just starting their journey, this program focuses on the fundamentals of graphic design using Adobe Photoshop. Students will learn key concepts, develop essential skills, and create a portfolio that showcases their newfound abilities.

6-Month Program:

This program is perfect for students looking to deepen their understanding of design principles. In addition to mastering Adobe Photoshop, participants will explore other design software and techniques. By the end, they will have a well-rounded portfolio that reflects their growth and creativity.

1-Year Program:

Our most comprehensive offering, this program provides an in-depth exploration of graphic design. Students will master Adobe Photoshop and other industry-standard tools, engage in real-world projects, and receive personalized mentorship. Graduates will emerge with a robust portfolio and the confidence to launch their careers in graphic design.
