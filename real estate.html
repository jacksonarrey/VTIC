3 Months Program

The 3-month program will begin with an introduction to real estate principles, focusing on foundational concepts and terminology through engaging lectures and interactive discussions, complemented by group activities and quizzes to reinforce learning. In the second month, participants will explore market dynamics, analyzing factors that influence real estate trends through case studies and practical market analysis projects. They will also learn property valuation methods in hands-on workshops, conducting comparative market analyses on actual properties. The final month will cover essential real estate law, where students will engage in case studies and negotiations to apply legal principles, followed by a focus on marketing strategies tailored for real estate, where they will create marketing plans utilizing digital and traditional techniques. This comprehensive approach combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing participants for success in the real estate industry.

6-Month Program:

The 6-month program will commence with an in-depth exploration of real estate principles, where participants will engage in comprehensive lectures and discussions to understand the complexities of the industry, supplemented by real-world examples and quizzes to reinforce knowledge. In the following month, the focus will shift to property management, covering essential skills such as tenant relations, maintenance, and financial management through interactive workshops and role-playing exercises. The third month will delve into real estate investment strategies, teaching participants how to evaluate investment opportunities, analyze risks, and develop a portfolio through market simulations and group projects. Legal and ethical issues will be addressed in the fourth month, utilizing case studies and discussions to navigate the legal landscape and ethical considerations in real estate transactions. The final two months will emphasize practical case studies, where participants will apply their accumulated knowledge to real-world scenarios, conducting analyses and presentations that showcase their understanding and problem-solving skills. This program combines theoretical foundations with practical applications to equip participants for a successful career in real estate.

1-Year Program:

The 1-year program will provide a comprehensive education in real estate management, beginning with a thorough exploration of comprehensive real estate management principles, where participants will engage in lectures and case studies to understand the multifaceted aspects of the industry. The second phase will focus on advanced property management, emphasizing best practices in tenant relations, maintenance, and financial oversight through hands-on workshops and real-world scenarios. In the third quarter, participants will delve into real estate development, learning about the planning, design, and execution of development projects, supported by guest speakers from the industry and site visits to active developments. The program will also cover finance and investment analysis, equipping students with the skills to evaluate investment opportunities and financial models through practical exercises and market analysis. An internship component will provide valuable hands-on experience, allowing participants to apply their knowledge in real-world settings while networking with professionals. Finally, the program culminates in a capstone project, where students will work in teams to develop a comprehensive real estate project plan, integrating their learning across all modules. This holistic approach ensures participants are well-prepared for a successful career in the real estate sector.
