WEB DEVELOPMENT IN VTIC

In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, mastering web development is crucial for individuals and businesses aiming to establish a strong online presence. Our school is committed to equipping students with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in web development. Our programs are designed to not only impart technical expertise but also to foster critical and creative thinking in the dynamic world of web design and development

Our Comprehensive Web Development Programs

6-Month Program:

this course incorporates WordPress and website management into the curriculum, offering a more comprehensive overview of web development strategies. Students will refine their skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript while gaining valuable insights into WordPress site creation and management. By the end of this program, participants will have developed a diverse skill set and a portfolio showcasing their capabilities.

1-Year Program:

Our most extensive offering, this program combines the first six months with a deep dive into advanced WordPress techniques, SEO, and comprehensive website management. Students will master web development skills, including advanced strategies in WordPress, along with best practices for SEO. Through hands-on projects and personalized mentorship, graduates will emerge with a robust portfolio and the confidence to thrive in the competitive web development field.
