VIDEO EDITING

VIDEO EDITING IN VTIC

Video editing is a vital skill for individuals and organizations looking to create engaging content that captures attention and communicates effectively. As a school, we recognize the increasing demand for proficient video editors who can bring stories to life through dynamic visuals. Our programs are designed to not only teach technical skills but also to inspire students to think creatively and critically about the art of editing

Our Comprehensive Video Editing Programs

>3-Month Program:

This introductory program is perfect for beginners eager to start their journey in video editing. Students will focus on the fundamentals using Adobe Premiere Pro, learning essential editing techniques and storytelling principles. By the end of the program, participants will have created a portfolio showcasing their newly acquired skills.

6-Month Program:

Designed for those who wish to deepen their editing expertise, this program expands on the foundational skills learned in the 3-month course. Students will delve into advanced editing techniques, explore sound design, and learn about motion graphics, all while working with Adobe Premiere Pro. Graduates will leave with a comprehensive portfolio that highlights their creative growth

1-Year Program:

Our most extensive offering, this program provides an in-depth exploration of video editing. Students will achieve mastery in Adobe Premiere Pro and other industry-standard tools, engage in real-world projects, and receive personalized mentorship from experienced professionals. By the end of the program, graduates will possess a robust portfolio and the confidence to embark on successful careers in video editing.
