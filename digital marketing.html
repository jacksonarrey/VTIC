DIGITAL MARKETING

DIGITAL MARKETING IN VTIC

In today's fast-paced digital world, effective marketing strategies are essential for businesses and organizations looking to connect with their target audiences. Our school is dedicated to equipping students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in digital marketing. Our programs are designed to not only teach technical expertise but also to inspire critical and creative thinking in the ever-evolving marketing landscape.

Our Comprehensive Digital Marketing Programs

3-Month Program: Introduction to Digital Marketing and Facebook Ads

This introductory program is perfect for beginners eager to explore the fundamentals of digital marketing. Students will learn essential concepts, strategies, and tools, with a focus on creating effective Facebook ad campaigns. By the end of the program, participants will have a foundational understanding of digital marketing and practical experience to kickstart their careers.

6-Month Program: Expanded Digital Marketing Skills

Building on the 3-month program, this course adds Google Ads to the curriculum, providing a more comprehensive overview of digital marketing strategies. Students will not only refine their Facebook advertising skills but also gain valuable insights into Google Ads campaigns. By the end of this program, participants will have developed a diverse set of skills and a portfolio showcasing their capabilities

1-Year Program:In-Depth Digital Marketing Mastery

Our most extensive offering, this program combines the first six months with a deep dive into Search Engine Optimization (SEO). Students will master digital marketing techniques, including advanced strategies in Facebook and Google Ads, along with SEO best practices. Through hands-on projects and personalized mentorship, graduates will emerge with a robust portfolio and the confidence to thrive in the digital marketing arena.
