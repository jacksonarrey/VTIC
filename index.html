VOCATIONAL TRAINING INSTITUTE FOR CHAMPIONS (VTIC)

Authorization Number: 000244/L/MINEFOP/SG/DFOP/SDGSF/CSACD

Vocational Training Institute for Champions is a Vocational and Certification Center found in Buea, Cameroon. It provides a conducive and enabling environment for students and professional to upgrade their skills and equally learn new skills. VTIC have 100% practicing professionals in her teaching list.

THE THREE MAIN SCHOOLS IN VTIC ARE:

School of Business Management ▼

The Business School of VTIC offers full time (one year) and part-time (three and six months) programs that are innovative, flexible careers and result focus. The entry level for each of these programs is your ability to read and write. In today’s business environment, teamwork and digital skills are essential to success. This program has incorporated these elements in the training of business students at VTIC. The program study in the school of business management include: Event Management, Real Estate Management, Business Coaching and Corporate Tax Management.

School of Information Technology ▼

The School of Information Technology equally offers both full-time (one year) and part-time (three and six months) programs that are interactive to meet up with the high demand of IT specialists in the world. The programs study in the school of Information Technology include; Digital Marketing, Web Design Management, Video Editing , General Microsoft Packages/Bilingual Secretarial duty.

VTIC DRVING SCHOOL ▼

At VTIC Driving School, we are dedicated to providing high-quality driving education that equips our students with the skills and confidence needed to become safe and responsible drivers. We issue driver's licenses in categories A, B, C, D, E, and F, and we offer professional training, short training courses, and practical-only sessions to meet the diverse needs of our students.
